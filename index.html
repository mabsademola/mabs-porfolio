Cazuba App

This is social messaging app that was developed and equipped with essential features to enhance your communication experience. With Cazuba, you can enjoy both video and audio calls, making it easy to connect with your friends and family in a more personal way. One standout feature of Cazuba is its message scheduling function, allowing you to plan and send messages at the perfect time, so you never forget important conversations or events. Moreover, Cazuba helps you find friends already using the app from your contact list, making it simple to connect with them. It also leverages Google Maps to display nearby users, similar to other popular messaging apps, facilitating easy meet-ups with friends in your vicinity. Behind the scenes, Cazuba is powered by cutting-edge technologies like Firebase, serverless functions, and Google Cloud, ensuring a seamless and secure experience for users. It's a user-friendly and feature-rich app designed to make social messaging more convenient and enjoyable for everyone.

Play All Games (PAG)

Play All Games (PAG) is a comprehensive gaming app with a vast collection of over 2000 online and offline games, a convenient space-saving feature for your phone. It offers a wide range of popular offline games like Tetris, Sudoku, and Ludo, along with various categories such as crossword puzzles, mechanical puzzles, logic puzzles, and word puzzles. Some standout features of Play All Games (PAG) include its diverse selection of addictive online and offline games, efficient storage management, captivating animations, and a user-friendly interface suitable for players of all ages. Play All Games (PAG)'s development is based on RestApi and sqlite, which ensure its smooth functioning and reliability. In addition to offering entertainment and challenges, the app underscores the educational value of puzzles in testing logic and reasoning skills, often encountered in exams. It's a versatile and enjoyable gaming solution for anyone looking to have fun and stimulate their mind.

About Me

Once upon a time, in the early stages of my life, I discovered the fascinating world of technology. It all began with a humble computer, and I was enthralled by the mystery of how those intricate games and applications were built. Little did I know that this initial curiosity would lead me on an incredible tech adventure. In 2018, I took a significant step forward and started delving into the world of mobile app development. Armed with determination and a mobile app called SKETCHWARE, I embarked on the journey of creating my own mobile applications. It was a thrilling experience that ignited my passion for coding. From there, I progressed to Java on Android Studio, dedicating the years 2019-2020 to honing my skills. But the path to success in the tech world isn't always smooth sailing. I faced my fair share of challenges, including a period of working remotely for a company that turned out to be a scam. Those two months were tough, but they taught me resilience. Another hurdle I encountered was wrestling with a subpar laptop during the early stages of my career. However, I was determined not to let these obstacles deter me. One of my proudest moments was working on projects like "Cazuba" and "Whitelist," where I not only designed captivating user interfaces but also wrote the code that brought these ideas to life. There's nothing quite like the satisfaction of knowing that thousands of people are using and benefiting from the products I've poured my heart into. Despite the odds stacked against me, especially considering the challenges of starting a tech career in Nigeria, I can confidently say that tech has been a transformative force in my life. I've learned that with dedication and a passion for technology, one can overcome any obstacle. From a humble beginning, I've journeyed into a world filled with endless possibilities, and the future looks brighter than ever. My story serves as a testament to the power of technology and the unwavering spirit of those who dare to dream big in the world of tech.
